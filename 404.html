awesome-crm
Open-source CRM for teams that outgrow spreadsheets

An enterprise-grade, free and self-hosted CRM built with NestJS and Next.js — leads & web-to-lead forms, a drag-and-drop sales pipeline, quotes & invoicing, chart-rich reports, signed webhooks, RBAC and multi-tenancy.

Features

🧲 Leads & intake

Unqualified leads, embeddable web-to-lead forms and HMAC-signed inbound webhooks with per-channel source tracking.

📊 Pipeline & Kanban

Drag-and-drop deals across configurable stages, collision-free ranking, activity log, lead → deal conversion.

👥 Contacts & companies

People and organizations, meetings, duplicate detection & merge.

🧾 Quotes & invoicing

CPQ quotes and invoices with immutable financial records, payments and server-side calculation.

📈 Reports & dashboard

Chart-rich reports: monthly revenue, win rate, sales by rep, top products, pipeline by stage.

🔗 Integrations & AI

Outbound HMAC-signed webhooks with delivery history, and an AI assistant (Claude).

🔐 RBAC & multi-tenancy

Two-layer permissions (roles + permissions), tenant isolation, audit log.

🌍 i18n & self-hosting

Built-in English & Turkish, add languages from the panel; one-command Docker deploy.

Why awesome-crm

  • Free & MIT-licensed — own your data, self-host anywhere.
  • API-first, layered architecture (Controller → Service → Repository).
  • Secure by default: every endpoint protected, financial integrity, signature-verified webhooks.
  • Tested: 106 unit + 124 e2e — including negative tests (IDOR, privilege escalation, signature tampering).
